how do I choose the right racing line?

Study the track layout, experiment during practice sessions, and prioritize the line that allows for the fastest exit speeds.

How can I improve my race craft?

Analyze race videos, learn from experienced drivers, and practice situational awareness and defensive driving techniques.

How do I improve my lap times?

To improve lap times, focus on refining your racing lines and maximizing corner exit speed. Additionally, optimizing braking points and fine-tuning throttle control can shave off valuable seconds on the track.

What's the role of aerodynamics in racing?

Aerodynamics affect cornering grip, straight-line speed, and stability, so optimizing aerodynamic components can significantly enhance performance.

How can I improve my consistency as a driver?

Practice regularly, analyze your driving data, and focus on maintaining consistent braking points, throttle application, and cornering techniques.
